Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Mr. Raheem Ahmed, Raheem Cooling began as a small family business with a vision to provide reliable and efficient air conditioning solutions to the local community. What started as a modest operation with just two technicians has grown into one of the region's most trusted HVAC service providers.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to include residential and commercial AC installations, maintenance, and repairs. Our commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth and success.
